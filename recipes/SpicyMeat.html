Spicy Meat Recipe

Ingredients needed for Spicy Meat

  • Eye of Round Roast
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Serrano or Habanero Peppers
  • Roma Tomatoes

Instructions to Cook

  1. Heat a pan over a medium-high heat
  2. As the pan heats up, dice the Eye of Round Roast
  3. Place desired salt and pepper in pan along with Roast
  4. Place Peppers and Tomatoes on top of meat to steam
  5. After 10 minutes the peppers and tomatoes will be finished steaming
  6. Blend peppers and tomatoes and add back into pan
  7. Let simmer for 5 - 10 minutes
  8. Enjoy!
