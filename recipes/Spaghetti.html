Spaghetti Recipe

Ingredients needed for Spaghetti

  • Ground Beef
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Hunts Meat Lover Tomato Sauce
  • Roma Tomatoes
  • Noodles

Instructions to Cook

  1. Heat a pan over a medium-high heat
  2. After the pan heats up, brown the beef
  3. While the beef is browning, get 6 cups of water to a boil in a pan
  4. When water is done boiling, place noodles in for 8 minutes
  5. When beef is brown, pour the tomato sauce into the pan and let heat
  6. Using a strainer, drain the water from noodles when ready. To test readiness, throw noodle at wall. If it sticks, it is ready.
  7. Combine the two finished products for Spaghetti
  8. Enjoy!
